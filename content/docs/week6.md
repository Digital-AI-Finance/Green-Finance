# Week 6: Global Regulatory Frameworks

## Learning Goals

1. **Understand the EU Taxonomy** and its six environmental objectives
2. **Apply SFDR classification** requirements to fund products
3. **Navigate global regulatory landscape** including ISSB standards

## Topics Covered

- EU Taxonomy framework
- SFDR Article 6, 8, 9 classification
- CSRD and double materiality
- ISSB standards and global adoption
- SEC climate disclosure rules
- Cross-jurisdictional comparison
