# Project Timeline

The GREEN FINANCE project runs for 36 months (December 2025 - November 2028), with activities organized across 5 Work Packages. Key milestones are linked to transnational meetings and major deliverables.

**Project Start (M1):** December 2025

## Progress Monitoring

### Bi-Monthly Online Calls
Regular consortium calls to:
- Track progress against milestones
- Address challenges and risks
- Align on upcoming activities
- Share best practices

### Bi-Annual Internal Reports
Partners submit progress reports covering:
- Grant usage
- Activity completion
- KPI achievement
- Risk updates

### Quality Assurance Reviews
- Internal QA Committee evaluations before each TPM
- External evaluator assessments at M18 and M36
- Partner satisfaction surveys at key milestones
