# Consortium Partners

The GREEN FINANCE consortium brings together 11 universities from 8 countries, combining European expertise in green finance with Asian market knowledge and implementation capacity.

---

## EU Partners

### Lucian Blaga University of Sibiu (ULBS) - Coordinator
**Romania**

{{< hint info >}}
**Role:** Project Coordinator
{{< /hint >}}

A globally ranked university and FORTHEM alliance member, ULBS specializes in economic sciences, IT, applied ecology, and mathematics. Its Faculty of Economic Sciences leads research in sustainable finance, ESG investing, and financial innovation.

**Expertise:** Project management, sustainable finance, ESG investing, financial research

**Website:** [ulbsibiu.ro](https://www.ulbsibiu.ro)

---

### University of Opole (OU)
**Poland**

A leader in economics and finance, OU specializes in financial innovations, education, and virtual currencies. Faculty expertise in blockchain and digital finance strengthens its contribution to innovative teaching methodologies.

**Expertise:** Financial innovations, blockchain, digital finance, personal finance, behavioral economics

**Website:** [uni.opole.pl](https://uni.opole.pl/en/)

---

### University of Twente (UTWENTE)
**Netherlands**

{{< hint info >}}
**Role:** WP3 Leader (Curriculum Development)
{{< /hint >}}

Combining technology, social sciences, and entrepreneurship, the University of Twente focuses on sustainable development and financial innovation. Its interdisciplinary approach advances green finance education and micro-credential training.

**Expertise:** Sustainable finance, AI in finance, fintech, data science, executive education

**Website:** [utwente.nl](https://www.utwente.nl)

---

### University of Siena (UNISI)
**Italy**

A historic institution with strong programs in economics, finance, and environmental sciences. Active in Erasmus+ projects including MICRO4ASIA, UNISI contributes expertise in micro-credentials and sustainable financial practices.

**Expertise:** Financial intermediaries, banking, insurance, fintech, ESG practices, micro-credentials

**Website:** [unisi.it](https://www.unisi.it)

---

### Institut de Haute Formation (IHF)
**Belgium**

{{< hint info >}}
**Role:** WP5 Co-Leader (Dissemination)
{{< /hint >}}

A Brussels-based institute specializing in EU policies, sustainability, and financial literacy. With 30+ years of experience and 150+ successful EU projects, IHF brings expertise in capacity building, dissemination, and EU project management.

**Expertise:** EU policy, project management, sustainability, financial literacy, dissemination

**Website:** [ihfeurope.eu](https://www.ihfeurope.eu)

---

## Asian Partners

### Banking University Ho Chi Minh City (BUH)
**Vietnam**

A premier banking university in Vietnam offering strong programs in finance and banking. Its focus on digital education and financial research supports green finance integration in the Vietnamese banking sector.

**Expertise:** Banking, finance, digital education, financial regulation, microfinance

**Website:** [hub.edu.vn](https://hub.edu.vn)

---

### Banking Academy of Vietnam (BAV)
**Vietnam**

A top finance and banking university with 600+ faculty members and 20,000 students annually. BAV's expertise spans banking, finance, and business management with strong connections to the State Bank of Vietnam.

**Expertise:** Banking, corporate finance, economic law, financial markets, macroeconomic policy

**Website:** [hvnh.edu.vn](https://hvnh.edu.vn)

---

### Payap University (PU)
**Thailand**

{{< hint info >}}
**Role:** WP5 Leader (Dissemination & Sustainability)
{{< /hint >}}

Committed to global engagement and sustainable finance, Payap University has participated in multiple Erasmus+ projects on green entrepreneurship and sustainable tourism. The Centre for Social Impact leads sustainability initiatives.

**Expertise:** Social enterprise, sustainable development, business ethics, green entrepreneurship

**Website:** [payap.ac.th](https://www.payap.ac.th)

---

### Walailak University (WU)
**Thailand**

A leading research university in Thailand excelling in sustainability and SDGs. Ranked among Asia's top universities for sustainable development, WU contributes expertise in economic sustainability and financial education.

**Expertise:** Economic sustainability, managerial economics, innovation management, ESG management

**Website:** [wu.ac.th](https://www.wu.ac.th)

---

### West Visayas State University (WVSU)
**Philippines**

A top Philippine research university with strengths in business management and ICT. Its focus on education quality and accessibility supports green finance integration in the Philippine higher education system.

**Expertise:** Business management, cooperative development, sustainable community development

**Website:** [wvsu.edu.ph](https://wvsu.edu.ph)

---

### Ateneo de Manila University (ADMU)
**Philippines**

{{< hint info >}}
**Role:** WP4 Leader (Programme Delivery)
{{< /hint >}}

A prestigious Jesuit university with strong programs in management, finance, and governance. The John Gokongwei School of Management leads the MS in Sustainability Management program, making it a key partner in green finance education.

**Expertise:** Finance, accounting, leadership, sustainability management, governance

**Website:** [ateneo.edu](https://www.ateneo.edu)

## Partnership Highlights

- **60% of project resources** allocated to Asian partners
- **Balanced leadership** - Asian partners lead WP4 and WP5
- **Regional coverage** - Partners in major economic centers across all three target countries
- **Complementary expertise** - EU partners bring green finance methodology; Asian partners bring market knowledge
