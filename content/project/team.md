## Governance Structure

### Steering Committee
One senior staff member from each partner organization (11 total), responsible for:
- Strategic decision-making
- Conflict resolution
- KPI monitoring
- External issue management

## External Evaluator

An independent external evaluator will be appointed through competitive selection to provide objective assessment of:
- Project effectiveness
- Implementation progress
- Stakeholder engagement
- Outcome sustainability

**Evaluations:** Mid-term (M18) and Final (M36)
