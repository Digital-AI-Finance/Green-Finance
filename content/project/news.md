# News & Updates

Stay informed about the latest developments in the GREEN FINANCE project.

---

## 2025

### August 2025 - Grant Agreement Signed

{{< hint info >}}
**Project Officially Launches**
{{< /hint >}}

The GREEN FINANCE consortium has officially signed the Grant Agreement with the European Education and Culture Executive Agency (EACEA). This marks the formal start of our 36-month journey to transform green finance education in Southeast Asia.

**Key Highlights:**
- Grant Agreement No. 101237817 signed
- 11 partner universities across 8 countries
- 3-year implementation period begins

---

### February 2025 - Proposal Submission

The GREEN FINANCE consortium successfully submitted its proposal to the Erasmus+ Capacity Building in Higher Education call (ERASMUS-EDU-2025-CBHE-STRAND-2).

**Proposal Highlights:**
- Comprehensive needs analysis conducted across Vietnam, Thailand, and Philippines
- Strong consortium with complementary expertise
- Innovative micro-credential approach to green finance education
