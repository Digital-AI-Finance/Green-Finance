# Week 3: ESG Integration

## Learning Goals

1. **Understand ESG rating methodologies** and provider landscape
2. **Analyze rating divergence** and its implications for portfolio construction
3. **Apply ESG screening strategies** to build sustainable portfolios

## Topics Covered

- Major ESG rating providers (MSCI, Sustainalytics, S&P)
- Rating methodology comparison
- Rating divergence analysis
- Screening strategies (negative, positive, best-in-class)
- ESG factor integration
- Portfolio construction techniques
