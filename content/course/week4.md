# Week 4: Climate Risk Assessment

## Learning Goals

1. **Understand climate risk frameworks** including TCFD and physical vs transition risks
2. **Apply carbon metrics** and temperature alignment methodologies
3. **Conduct scenario analysis** for portfolio climate risk assessment

## Topics Covered

- TCFD framework and recommendations
- Physical risks vs transition risks
- Carbon footprinting (Scope 1, 2, 3)
- Climate Value-at-Risk
- Temperature alignment metrics
- NGFS scenarios
