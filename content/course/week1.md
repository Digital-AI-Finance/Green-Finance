# Week 1: Green Finance Fundamentals

## Learning Goals

1. **Understand the theoretical foundations** of sustainable finance and its role in addressing climate change
2. **Quantify market developments** through analysis of green bond growth, regional distribution, and issuer composition
3. **Apply analytical frameworks** to evaluate green finance instruments and investment opportunities

## Topics Covered

- Definition and scope of green finance
- Historical development and market growth
- Key instruments: green bonds, sustainability-linked bonds, transition bonds
- Regulatory landscape overview
- Investment gap analysis

## Key Statistics

- Global green bond market: $2.9 trillion (2024)
- CAGR 2015-2024: 28.1%
- Regional distribution: Europe 45%, APAC 25%, Americas 25%
