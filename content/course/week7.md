# Week 7: Impact Investing

## Learning Goals

1. **Understand impact measurement frameworks** including IRIS+ and SROI
2. **Align investments with SDGs** using systematic mapping approaches
3. **Evaluate natural capital** and biodiversity considerations

## Topics Covered

- IRIS+ impact metrics
- Social Return on Investment (SROI)
- SDG alignment mapping
- Impact measurement and management
- Natural capital accounting
- Biodiversity finance
