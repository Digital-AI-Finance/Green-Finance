# Week 2: Green Bonds

## Learning Goals

1. **Understand green bond frameworks** including GBP, CBI, and EU GBS standards
2. **Analyze greenium dynamics** and market pricing of green bonds
3. **Apply due diligence frameworks** to evaluate green bond investments

## Topics Covered

- Green Bond Principles (ICMA)
- Climate Bonds Initiative certification
- EU Green Bond Standard
- Greenium analysis and evolution
- Sustainability-Linked Bonds (SLBs)
- Use of proceeds verification

## Key Statistics

- Greenium range: 2-7 bps (declining over time)
- CBI certification: 80%+ verification rate
- SLB growth: 40% CAGR since 2019
